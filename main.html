Maldives - A Tropical Paradise


The Maldives is an island country located in the Indian Ocean, known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and overwater bungalows. It is a popular destination for honeymooners, scuba divers, and beach lovers.

  1. Snorkeling and Diving - The Maldives is home to some of the most diverse and colorful marine life in the world. Snorkeling and diving are popular activities, with many dive sites accessible from the shore or via boat.
  2. Island Hopping - With over 1,000 islands to explore, island hopping is a must-do activity in the Maldives. Each island has its own unique charm, from local villages to luxury resorts.
  3. Male City - The capital city of the Maldives is a bustling hub of activity, with colorful markets, historical landmarks, and lively restaurants and cafes.
  4. Spa and Wellness - Many resorts in the Maldives offer spa and wellness services, including massages, yoga classes, and meditation sessions. It's the perfect way to unwind and relax in paradise.
  5. Sunset Cruise - Watching the sunset over the Indian Ocean is a breathtaking experience. Many resorts offer sunset cruises, where you can enjoy a glass of champagne and soak in the stunning views.
  • Hedhikaa- A type of Maldivian snack made from fish, coconut, and spices.
  • Mas Huni - A breakfast dish made from shredded tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Garudhiya - A traditional fish soup made with lime, chili, and onions.
  • Bis Keemiya - A pastry filled with spiced meat and vegetables.
  • Fihunu Mas - Grilled fish marinated in chili, garlic, and ginger.
